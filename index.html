Join The Clean Bunch

Welcome to The Clean Bunch, a group of passionate individuals dedicated to keeping Monaghan clean and free of litter. Our mission is to inspire others to join us in taking action against litter and promote a cleaner and healthier environment for all. We believe that every small action can make a big impact, and by working together, we can make a difference. Join us in our efforts to create a cleaner Monaghan.

Help The Community

The Clean Bunch's litter picking efforts have a positive impact on the community in many ways. Firstly, removing litter from public spaces such as Rossmore park and the streets of Monaghan makes them more pleasant and inviting for everyone to enjoy. It can also help to reduce the risk of injury and disease caused by hazardous waste and sharp objects. Secondly, a cleaner community can promote a sense of pride and ownership amongst residents, which can lead to more responsible and sustainable behaviour in the future.

Help The Environment

The Clean Bunch's litter picking activities are not only beneficial to the community but also have a significant positive impact on the environment. Litter, especially plastics, takes a long time to decompose, and it poses a severe threat to the environment and wildlife. By removing litter from our environment, we reduce the risk of harmful chemicals leaching into our soil and waterways.The Clean Bunch's efforts to reduce litter not only improve the aesthetic quality of the environment, but they also promote a healthier and more sustainable environment for wildlife and future generations.

Exercise

Litter picking is an excellent form of exercise that offers numerous health benefits. It is an excellent way to get some fresh air and spend time outdoors, which can boost your mood and reduce stress levels. Litter picking involves bending, stretching, and walking, which can help improve flexibility, strengthen muscles, and increase cardiovascular endurance. Additionally, litter picking requires focus and concentration, which can help improve cognitive function and reduce the risk of age-related cognitive decline.

Socialize

Litter picking provides an excellent opportunity for people to socialize while doing something positive for the community and the environment. Whether it is a formal organized event or just a few friends getting together, litter picking can be a fun and social activity. Picking up litter allows individuals to work together, chat, and share stories, creating a sense of community. It also provides an opportunity to meet new people who share similar interests, leading to new friendships and networks.
